Questions, honestly answered.

What does Aaj Kya Khaun actually do?

It's an AI agent that knows what's in your kitchen — by reading your last 30 Zepto orders — and answers two questions: "What should I eat from what I have?" and "I want X, what's missing?". When something is missing, it places a Zepto order in chat. COD by default.

How is this different from Zepto's own app?

Zepto delivers; we decide. Their app shows a catalog and waits for you to scroll. We start from your kitchen — what you already have, what you ate recently — and only surface what you're actually missing. One sentence in, an order out.

Do I need a Zepto account?

Yes. We connect via Zepto's official OAuth — you sign in once with your Zepto account, the token stays encrypted on our server, and we use Zepto's API to search products and place orders for you. We never see your Zepto password.

What does it cost?

Free during beta. Zepto's prices and delivery fees are unchanged — we don't mark anything up.

Is my data safe?

Tokens are encrypted at rest. We don't sell data, don't run ads, don't share your inventory with anyone. Cancel any time and we delete everything.

Do I have to use Telegram?

No. The chat works directly on the web (with optional push notifications via PWA) or via Telegram — your choice during onboarding. You can switch anytime.

Where do you deliver?

Anywhere Zepto delivers. We use the addresses already saved on your Zepto account; you can pick which one when ordering.
