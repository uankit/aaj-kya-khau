🔬 Built on peer-reviewed research

Science over guesswork.

Nutrition math isn't made up. Every calorie and macro target comes from the definitive Indian nutrition literature and validated global standards.

01

Mifflin-St Jeor Equation

Am J Clin Nutr, 1990

Most accurate BMR formula for general populations. Used to calculate your baseline calorie burn.

02

ICMR-NIN RDA 2020

National Institute of Nutrition, Hyderabad

India-specific macronutrient recommendations for protein, carbs, fat, and fiber.

03

Indian Food Composition Tables 2017

Longvah T et al., NIN-ICMR

Lab-analyzed nutritional data for 500+ Indian foods. This is where every "paneer has 18g protein per 100g" comes from.

04

WHO Technical Report 916

World Health Organization, 2003

Weight management targets: 500 cal/day deficit for sustainable loss, surplus for gain.
